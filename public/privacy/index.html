Notes

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2026

Notes is a personal note-taking app built around a simple promise: your content stays on your device. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

The short version: The app has no user accounts and no backend server that stores your data. Your notes, voice memos, photos, files and checklists are saved locally on your device and are never uploaded to us or anyone else.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We have no user accounts, logins, or sign-up.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not have a server that receives or stores your notes.

Where your data is stored

Everything you create in the app — text notes, voice recordings, transcripts, photos, file attachments, checklists, pins, and theme preferences — is stored entirely on your device using your browser's local storage (localStorage and IndexedDB). It remains accessible only on that device. If you uninstall the app or clear its storage, that data is permanently deleted.

Network connections

The app makes a small number of outbound requests to function. These download resources to your device; they do not send your notes anywhere:

  • App delivery (Vercel): loading the app itself.
  • Fonts (Google Fonts): loading the typefaces used in the interface.
  • Daily quote (ZenQuotes): fetching one inspirational quote per day. No personal data is sent.
  • On-device AI search models (Hugging Face CDN): downloading the search models, which then run entirely on your device. Your search queries and notes are processed locally and are never transmitted.

As with any internet request, these services may receive standard technical information such as your device's IP address, used only to deliver the requested content. We do not control these third parties; their handling of such data is governed by their own privacy policies.

Device permissions

The app may request access to your microphone (to record voice memos) and your camera or photos (to attach images). These are used only when you choose to use those features, and the resulting recordings and images are stored only on your device.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at fastium.support@gmail.com.
